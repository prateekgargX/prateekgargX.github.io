I recently graduated with a dual degree (B.Tech + M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering and Machine Learning from the Centre for Machine Intelligence and Data Science at IIT Bombay.

I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sunita Sarawagi. I am broadly interested in generative modelling and its applications. Part of my work at IIT Bombay was accepted at ICLR 2025.

Since graduation, I’m working as research assistant at Aalto University under Prof. Vikas Garg, developing new variants of Discrete Diffusion models.

I am interested in developing practical and efficient models that can support a wide range of inference tasks, as well as in exploring robust evaluation methods to better understand their capabilities and limitations.

You can find a list of my publications here, and explore my projects on my GitHub.
